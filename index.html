The honest version

I'm a grad student and ML researcher who got tired of research feeling inaccessible. Papers are dense. Explanations online are either too shallow or too academic. That gap is where most people give up — and it's where I like to write.

That same instinct led me to start The CorporateWorms — an edtech startup focused on making domain mastery genuinely achievable for people who are serious about getting good.

What I do
🔬
ML Researcher
Exploring LLMs, memory systems, and efficient inference.
🎓
Educator
Teaching ML to learners who want to actually understand it.
🚀
CEO
Running The CorporateWorms, an edtech startup.
🧭
Mentor
Guiding learners toward data science domain mastery.
What I'm thinking about

Right now I'm deep in LLM systems — memory efficiency, long-context tradeoffs, inference costs. The unsexy parts that actually determine what gets built.
